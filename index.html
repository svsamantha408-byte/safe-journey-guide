Safety Tips from Officer Riley

🛑

Stop at Signals

Always respect traffic lights and stop signs — even when the road looks empty.

📱

No Phone Driving

Texting while driving multiplies your crash risk by 23×. Use hands-free only.

🌧️

Slow in Rain

Wet roads reduce tyre grip. Cut speed by 30% and double your following distance.

🚗

Buckle Up

Seatbelts reduce fatal injury risk by 45% — every passenger, every seat.

😴

Rest Every 2 Hours

Drowsy driving is as dangerous as drunk driving. Stop and stretch.

🍺

Don't Drink & Drive

Even small amounts of alcohol impair reaction time. Plan a safe ride home.

Why SafeRoute?

Road accidents claim 1.35 million lives globally each year. SafeRoute helps drivers make safer choices by visualizing route risk, surfacing weather hazards, and reinforcing safe driving habits — all in one friendly place.
